About

I started my career as a software developer at Broadridge Financial Solutions, building enterprise-grade Workday integrations for systems processing 100K+ records monthly. Outside my corporate work, I mentor youth in tech through Year Up and Technovation, and am building a stealth AI startup — because I believe technology should create things that genuinely matter to people.
